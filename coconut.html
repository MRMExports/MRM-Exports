Coconut

Pioneers in the industry, we offer Desiccated & Dry Coconut such as Dry Coconut and Desiccated Coconut from India.
Our coconuts are known for their sweet water, thick fleshy cream and unique natural sweet taste. We export coconuts with and without husks. We get our coconuts from reliable farmers who cultivate them in environmental friendly conditions. The coconuts have a distinct flavour. The fully matured coconuts are light brown in colour. Coconut is used in sweet dishes as well as savouries.
